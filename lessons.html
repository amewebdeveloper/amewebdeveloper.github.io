Lesson Information

We are now registering for group lessons in the Ness County, Kansas area.

Group A: Ages 6-9 (1st Grade-4th Grade)

Group B: Ages 10-17 (5th Grade-High school)

Group C: Adults (TBD)


Adults: If you are interested in learning an instrument, please let us know. Based on interest we can open a group for adults, or individual lessons as needed.


Lesson Rates:

Group lessons (2+ students must be present to be a group): $20/hour

Individual lesson:

       $20/half-hour
       $30/45-minutes
       $40/hour
  • In-person or Zoom/Skype lessons are available
  • In-person lessons will take place at the Seventh-Day Adventist Church in Bazine, KS.
  • Beginning students will find a half-hour lesson to be sufficient. Longer lesson times are needed for more advanced students.

Note: Individual lesson spots will be limited and will be available only to:

  • students with prior experience on their string instrument
  • students who have attended at least one season of group lessons and would like to increase/refine their skills
  • adults who are unable to attend a group session
